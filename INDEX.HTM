WHAT IS COMPUTER

A computer is an electronic machine that works under the instructions given by you. It can accept data (input), manipulate data according to specified rules (process), produce results (output), and store the result for future use.

Today, in the age of electronic revolution the computer has become the intergral part of our life. We can say that computers have entered into each and every walk of life either it is home, at work or at school. Most of our daily activities either involve the use of or depend upon the information derived from a computer.

Many schools, colleges and homes have computers for educational purposes. Student complete assignments and do research on computers in lab rooms. Computers have become a source of entertainment.

You can play games, listen to music, watch a video or a movie, read a book magazine, compose a video, re-touch a photograph, plan a vacation or do many more.
